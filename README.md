# NovaRetail+ Customer Behavior Analysis

Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) of customer behavior data from **NovaRetail+**, an e-commerce platform. The goal is to identify which customer behavior variables are most closely associated with **annual customer revenue**.

> **Note:** This project focuses on correlation analysis and does not establish causal relationships.

---

## Project Overview

This notebook includes:

- Data cleaning and preprocessing
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Correlation analysis
- Data visualization
- Business insights and recommendations

---

## Dataset

The dataset contains approximately **15,000 customer records** with demographic, behavioral, and business-related features, including:

- Customer age and income
- Monthly visits and purchases
- Marketing spend
- Satisfaction score
- Premium membership
- Churn status
- Annual revenue (target variable)

## Key Findings

- Monthly purchases have the strongest positive correlation with annual revenue.
- Customer visits are moderately related to purchasing behavior.
- Premium membership and customer satisfaction provide additional business insights.
- Results should be interpreted as correlations, not causal relationships.
